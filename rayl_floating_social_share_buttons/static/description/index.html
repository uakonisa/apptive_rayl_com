You can display share buttons on social media on your website using the floating sidebar. Buttons for sharing on social networks easily customizable.

How this addons work?

Fast Setting.

Setiing position of the floating bar in windows.

  1. Select where the floating bar is displayed on the right or left of the screen
  2. Specify a number. Increase in number will shift sharing interface towards right and decrease will shift it towards left. Number can be negative too.
  3. Specify a number. Increase in number will shift sharing interface towards bottom and decrease will shift it towards top.
  4. Hide button to share with other social networks
  5. Shape of the icons in the floating bar, Circle or Square

6.- Popup to select hierarchy of social network buttons. Priority position

8.- Bar Floating Color Buttons; Classic Color or Color Theme active in Odoo
